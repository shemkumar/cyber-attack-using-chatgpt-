Defense Strategies

Explore how AI and generative models can be used to defend against cyber threats and mitigate risks.

1. Automated Defense Mechanisms

Generative models can be used to automatically generate defense strategies, such as security patches, firewalls, and intrusion detection systems, based on attack patterns.

2. Intrusion Detection Systems

AI models can learn from vast datasets of cyber attacks to identify patterns and anomalies in network traffic, helping to detect and block unauthorized access attempts in real-time.

3. Threat Intelligence Sharing

By using generative models, organizations can create and share threat intelligence in an automated manner, making it easier to detect and respond to emerging threats.
