Threat Analysis

Research and analysis into cyber threats and how generative models can simulate attack scenarios.

1. Risk Assessment

We analyze the potential risks posed by generative models in the hands of malicious actors. Understanding how AI could be misused is key to developing effective defense strategies.

2. Attack Simulation

Generative models are used to simulate various types of cyber attacks to understand their potential impact. These simulations help security teams prepare for real-world attacks.

3. Adaptive Defense Analysis

Using adaptive defense models, we analyze how defense strategies can evolve in response to ongoing cyber attacks, ensuring that security remains robust and effective over time.
